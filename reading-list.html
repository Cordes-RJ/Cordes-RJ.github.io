Just a list of works I've read over the years in chronological order. I haven't always kept track properly so there are many missing pieces. I only list works which I believe seriously reversed, upgraded, or otherwise impacted my thinking--this being said, not all listed titles should be taken as endorsements as some simply upgraded my understanding of counters to my own thinking. If a title is listed at a time beyond the current date, it is because this is when I expect to complete reading the title by.
